3 Historical Fun Facts

Ancient Settlement

Polynesians first traveled across open waters and settled Bora Bora around 1,500 years ago.

World War II Base

The United States constructed a strategic military base on Bora Bora during World War II.

French Territory

Bora Bora is an integral part of French Polynesia, an overseas collectivity territory of France.
